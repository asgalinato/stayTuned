StayTuned

Our Mission: We are dedicated to enhancing user experiences through innovative digital solutions. StayTuned is designed to help businesses and individuals gain insightful feedback from text data efficiently. It's technology leverages machine learning techniques to interpret and analyze sentiments expressed in written content.

Our Technology: At StayTuned, Robustly Optimized Bidirectional Encoder Representations from Transformers Approach (roBERTa) model is utilized to determine sentiments of textual data. This process involves the computational identification and categorization of opinions expressed in a piece of text, especially in order to determine whether the writer's attitude towards a particular topic, product, or service is positive, neutral, or negative.
